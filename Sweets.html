CHOCOLATE LAVA BAKED OATS

INGREDIENTS

1 banana
2/3 c. milk
2/3 c. oats
1 egg
1 tbsp. maple syrup
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tbsp. peanut butter
1 tbsp. cocoa
dark chocolate chips
pinch of salt

INSTRUCTIONS:

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Cover oven safe baking dish with cooking spray. Mash the banana using a fork and combine remaining ingredients. Place into an oven safe baking dish. Bake for 17-20 minutes. Serve warm and enjoy!

MOM'S CHOCOLATE CHIP BANANA BREAD

INGREDIENTS

3-4 overripe bananas
3/4 c. sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 c. vegetable oil
1 1/2 c. all purpose flour
1 1/4 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/8 tsp. salt
1 c. chocolate chips
dash of ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS:

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Prepare a 9x5 baking pan by spraying it with nonstick spray. Mash the bananas with a fork. Mix in sugar, egg, vanilla, and vegetable iol. Mix in the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt. Fold in the chocolate chips. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan (and top with more chocolate chips if you want). Bake for 55-60 minutes. Serve warm and enjoy!
