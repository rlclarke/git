ONE POT ROASTED RED PEPPER AND ALFREDO PASTA

INGREDIENTS

3/4 lb Italian sausage
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 tbsp olive oil
1 1/2 cups milk, plus 1/4 cup for sauce
1 1/2 cups chicken broth, plus 1/4 cup for sauce
16 oz jar roasted red peppers, drained and pureed
4 oz goat cheese
1/2 cup grated parmesan
3 handfuls fresh spinach
12oz pasta of your choice
salt & pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS:

In a large pan, saute the sausage with the garlic and olive oil over medium heat until browned.Stir in the chicken broth, pureed roasted red peppers, milk and pasta. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until pasta is cooked through, about 12 minutes. Stir in spinach, 1 handful at a time and cook until wilted. Stir in goat and parmesan cheese until melted. Serve warm and enjoy!

CREAMY NOODLE BOWL WITH RAINBOW VEGGIES

INGREDIENTS

1 cup cashews
1 English cucumber
1 red bell pepper
10oz soba noodles
1/4 cup peanut butter
2 tbsp honey
2 tbsp water
4 tsp soy sauce
4 tsp rice vinegar
1 tsp chili-garlic sauce
1 tsp toasted seasame oil

INSTRUCTIONS:

Bring a large pot to a boil. While the water boils wash, dry and chop produce. Add the peanut butter, honey, water, soy sauce, vinegar, chili-garlic sauce and sesame oil a bowl and whisk until smooth, set aside. Once water is boiling, cook the noodles according to instruction. When cooked, drain and rinse well under cold water. Roughly chop the cashews. Add noodles, cashews, produce and sauce to a bowl and toss to combine. Serve cold and enjoy!

CHICKPEA SALAD RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

15 oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
8 oz orzo pasta, cooked
1 English cucumber
1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
5-6 green onions
1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
For salad dressing:
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 cup lemon, juiced
3 tbsp red wine vinegar
salt & pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS:

Cook orzo according to package until al dente. Run under cold water when pasta is done cooking. In a large bowl combine all the salad ingredients including the orzo. In a mason jar, combine the salad dressing ingredients and whisk or shake to combine. Pour the salad dressing over the salad and mix well. Serve cold and enjoy!
