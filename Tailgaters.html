BRIE & CRANBERRY CROISSANT ROLL

INGREDIENTS

1 roll of crescent dough sheet
1 circle of brie cheese
1 can whole berry cranberry sauce
1 egg

INSTRUCTIONS:

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Unroll crescent dough and set the brie in the middle. Top with some cranberry sauce. Fold the dough up and over the brie. Brush the top with the egg wash and bake for 35-40 minutes. Serve hot and enjoy!

PULLED ZESTY BBQ CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS

4 chicken breats
1.5 c. BBQ sauce
1/2 c. Italian dressing
1/4 c. brown sugar
2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 chicken bouillon cubes, crushed

INSTRUCTIONS:

Place chicken breasts in the crock pot. Combine the BBQ sauce, Italian dressing, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and chicken bouillon. Pour mixture over chicken in the crock pot. Cook on high for 4 hours or low for 7-8 hours. Serve hot and enjoy!

CROCKPOT MAC & CHEESE

INGREDIENTS

2 c. cooked elbow macaroni
2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 c. shredded provolone cheese
1/4 c. parmesan cheese
1/2 c. sour cream
4 oz. cream cheese
14 oz. can of evaporated milk
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. dry mustard

INSTRUCTIONS:

Spray a large 5-6 quart crock pot with cooking spray. Cook two cups of dry elbow macaroni according to directions, leaving al dente, about 3-4 minutes shy of being done. Drain and rinse with cold water. Place macaroni in the crock pot. Add in the cheeses, sour cream, cream cheese, mayo and seasonings. Stir to combine. Pour evaporated milk over the top of the mixture. Cook on low for 2 hours, stirring half way through. Serve hot and enjoy!
